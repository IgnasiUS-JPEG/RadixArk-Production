The 70 FPS Standard: Why Performance is the Ultimate User Experience

Users rarely say “thank you” for performance—but they always feel its absence.

We argue that 70 FPS is the new baseline for interactive systems, and walk through the architectural and design decisions required to get there.

From render pipelines to perceived latency tricks, this piece is a tour of the performance mindset inside RadixArk.
