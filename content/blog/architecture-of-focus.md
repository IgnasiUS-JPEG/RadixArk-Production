Great products are not the ones with the most surface area; they are the ones with the highest density of meaning per pixel.

This article breaks down how to design interfaces that respect attention: from contrast and motion to information hierarchy and micro‑copy.

The result is not minimalism for its own sake, but a product that feels fast, clear, and quietly powerful.
