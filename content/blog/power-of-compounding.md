Compounding is not just a financial idea; it is a product idea. The teams that win are the ones that design feedback loops instead of one‑off launches.

Compounding shows up in onboarding, activation, retention, infrastructure, and pricing—and small, consistent improvements beat occasional heroic sprints.

If you are building long‑lived products, this shift in perspective changes how you prioritize almost everything.
