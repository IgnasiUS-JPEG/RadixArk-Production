The best automation does not feel like automation at all; it feels like a collaborator who anticipates what you are trying to do.

This article explores how to express high‑level logic in interfaces without overwhelming people, and how AI can stay legible, steerable, and trustworthy.

If you are designing with intelligent systems, this piece is a field guide to keeping humans in the loop in a respectful way.
